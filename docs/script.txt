INT. KITCHEN - NIGHT

The room is dimly lit by a warm practical lamp on the right. Rain streaks the window on the left.

PROTAGONIST A sits at the wood table in the center. He wears a white hoodie and black jeans. He spins a silver ring on his left hand.

A RED MUG sits on the table in front of him. Steam rises from it.

PROTAGONIST A
(whispering)
It's quiet. Too quiet.

He reaches for the RED MUG but hesitates. The lights flicker.

EXT. STREET - NIGHT

Rain pours down on the pavement. A black car drives past slowly.
